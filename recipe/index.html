Pepperoni Pizza

Ingredients

  • 1 can (8oz each) Hunt's Tomatoe Sauce-No Salt Added
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 prebaked thin pizza crust (12-inch)
  • 1/3 cup sliced pepperoni

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees.
  2. Combine tomato sauce, Parmesan cheese and oregano in small bowl.
  3. Spread sauce evenly over crust.
  4. Sprinkle with mozzarella cheese; top with pepperoni.
  5. Place on inrgeased pizza pan.
  6. Bale 12 to 15 minutes or until edges of crust are browned lightly and cheese melts.
  7. Cut pizza into 6 slices.
